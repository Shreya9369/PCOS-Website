Why Workouts Are Important for PCOS

Exercise plays a powerful role in managing PCOS symptoms. It improves insulin sensitivity, supports weight management, reduces stress, and helps balance hormones.

Women with PCOS often struggle with hormonal imbalances, fatigue, and mood swings. Regular physical activity, especially gentle and mindful routines like yoga, walking, and Pilates, can significantly reduce these symptoms.
